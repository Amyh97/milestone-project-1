Profile

I tried progressing my career with a previous employer as their head office is also in Bracknell, which got me thinking, what do I really want to do? As much as I enjoyed working for that company, I realised that I wanted to make the most of my passion for IT, which I could not do working where I did. I often thought about working in the IT industry but was not sure what most of the jobs meant, but I could understand the benefits of web design and web development. I also like being able to see the finished product of my work, which this will allow me to do.

Once I had decided on web design/development it was clear that I was going to need to complete training before starting out in the industry, which led me to finding the Full Stack Development Diploma with Code Institute. I planned to train part-time alongside working full time. However, at the time of doing the Full Stack Development Diploma, I was put on temporary leave from work due to the COVID-19 outbreak. This mean that was able to focus on this full time, allowing me to make best use of my time off and gain the skills required for the industry as quickly as I could.

On completion of the Diploma I would like to go into web-design and web-development. I want to create beautiful websites that are not only visually appealing, but have an excellent UX to help businesses achieve their goals buy keeping users on the sites as long as possible by using intuitive designs that encourage users to explore the site. I wish to help a large variety of companies, small and large, to achieve their potential. I feel a good online presence and website are key to the success of a business, with the internet becoming a bigger part of our everyday lives.
